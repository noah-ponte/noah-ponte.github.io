Aperture

How wide the opening on the lens is

Another name for this is f-stop and is labeled as f. or f/ and a number

The lower the value of the number the wider the opening of the lens

Depth of Field is also controlled by Aperture

Lower aperture means wider opening and shallow depth of field(blurry background)

Higher aperture means smaller opening and deeper depth of field(blurry foreground)
